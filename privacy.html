Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. CartMind is committed to protecting your personal data with transparency, security, and care.

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Introduction & Scope

Welcome to CartMind ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.cartmind.com, use our smart cart services, or interact with our platform. Please read this policy carefully. By using CartMind, you consent to the data practices described herein.

We value your privacy and comply with applicable regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other global standards. This policy applies to all users, visitors, and customers.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with a seamless and intelligent shopping experience, CartMind collects the following categories of information:

Personal Identifiers

Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, and account credentials.

Payment Information

Encrypted payment details (processed via PCI-compliant partners, never stored directly).

Usage & Device Data

IP address, browser type, device IDs, pages visited, cart interactions, and shopping preferences.

Communications

Support chats, reviews, feedback, and survey responses.

We also collect Smart Cart insights (such as saved items, price alerts) to enhance your experience. All data is gathered lawfully and transparently.

3. How We Use Your Information

CartMind uses your data to operate, improve, and personalize our services. Key purposes include:

  • ✓ Processing orders, payments, and delivering products.
  • ✓ Providing AI-driven cart recommendations and tailored promotions.
  • ✓ Communicating order updates, returns, and customer support.
  • ✓ Preventing fraud, securing accounts, and ensuring platform integrity.
  • ✓ Analyzing trends to optimize our website and marketing efforts.
  • ✓ Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service.

We never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Any sharing is limited and described in Section 5.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / Global)

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA) or similar jurisdictions, we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: to fulfill orders and provide requested services.
  • Legitimate interests: to improve CartMind, prevent fraud, and conduct analytics.
  • Consent: for marketing communications (you can withdraw anytime).
  • Legal obligations: to comply with tax, anti-fraud, or regulatory requirements.

5. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your data with limited third parties only for legitimate business purposes:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), shipping carriers (FedEx, USPS), hosting and analytics (AWS, Google Analytics).
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred with notice.
  • Legal Compliance: To respond to subpoenas, court orders, or governmental requests.
  • Protection of Rights: To enforce our policies, investigate fraud, or protect safety.

All third parties are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

CartMind uses cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Types we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for cart operations, login, and checkout.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior (Google Analytics, privacy-friendly).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language and region settings.
  • Marketing Cookies: Limited to retargeting with your consent (optional).

You can opt out of non-essential cookies via our cookie consent banner when you first visit.

7. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, role-based access controls, regular vulnerability scans, and secure data centers. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary: account data until you close your account, transaction data for up to 7 years (tax/accounting), and usage analytics for 2 years. You may request deletion as described in Section 8.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion (Right to be Forgotten): Request erasure of your data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Opt-out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails via link or contacting us.
  • Restriction & Objection: Limit how we process your data in certain situations.
  • Do Not Sell My Info (CCPA): CartMind does not sell personal data; you can verify anytime.

To exercise any right, email privacy@cartmind.com or use our privacy request portal (available in account settings). We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

CartMind does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 16. If we discover that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. Parents/guardians may contact us to raise concerns.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. For users in the EEA, we ensure adequate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place. By using CartMind, you consent to such transfers.

11. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or prominent notice on our site. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the latest revision. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
